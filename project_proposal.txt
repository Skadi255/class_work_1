Project Proposal 

How did COVID-19 affect the housing market across Texas?
Did COVID lead to a housing market boom?   How did house sales change from 2018-2019 to 2020-2021?
How did interest rates change pre-covid and post-covid?
How was house price affected by Covid?  What is the change in square foot price in real estate?
How did job opportunities change pre and post Covid?  Were more remote work job opportunities available? 
Was housing price affected by supply materials and shipping?  How did supply materials and shipping prices change, pre and post Covid?
